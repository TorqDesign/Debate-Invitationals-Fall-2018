What is Massey Debate Invitationals

Massey Debate Invitationals is a high school debate competition that takes place at Vincent Massey Secondary School. Debators of different skill levels from across the Windsor-Essex area come together for a day to brush up on their debating skills and have a great time! Our 2018 fall MDI is on the 24th of November , you can sign up online with a partner and pay the registration fee of 10 bucks upon entrance. Hope to see you there!
