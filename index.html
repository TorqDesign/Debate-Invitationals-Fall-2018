MasseyHacks

Spring 2019 @ Vincent Massey S.S.


What is MasseyHacks?


MasseyHacks is a high school event where 250+ students of all skill levels — from those who haven't written "Hello World!" to seasoned hackers with SSH enabled power bars — come together to learn and innovate for 24 hours straight. Provided resources include lots of hardware for great engineering hacks, intelligent and experienced mentors to provide an enriching educational experience and several side events to keep everyone entertained through the night. In summary, MasseyHacks is a place to immerse yourself in the massive world of computer science and engineering, create any tech you want and let your imagination run free.

"The amount of free swag + the enthusiasm of the organizers was great.
Honestly, the entire weekend felt extremely well-organized and made for a great first hackathon."

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people, usually students, of any background and discipline come together to immerse themselves in a condensed period of computer science-infused activity, from tech workshops to learning code to building amazing projects of limitless creativity.

Do I need to know how to program?

Not at all — in fact, newcomers are encouraged to come and participate in hackathons in general. There, we’ll teach you the fundamentals of code, programmer essentials and lots more!

What should I bring?

Bring your own laptop and charger, as we can’t provide machines for everyone. Power strips are recommended. Sleeping apparatus and toiletries are also a good idea. As for other necessities like food, freedom and fun, rest assured that we’ve got you covered.

How will the teams work?

You can always hack by yourself, but teams are usually preferred. Teams are composed of no more than 4 people. You’re encouraged to form your own team for the event beforehand, but we will have a team formation period where you can find other hackers to work with for the event.

I know a company that wants to sponsor, what should I tell them?

Tell them thanks, firstly. Then ask them to email us at sponsorship@masseyhacks.ca to get started!

How much does it cost?

MasseyHacks charges nothing at all for entry — the entire event is free for attendees!

How do I know if I got accepted?

We will know in time... Lemme ask the director. Everything is a mystery, and that keeps the fun in things.

How do I get to MasseyHacks V?

We’re situated in Windsor, Ontario, Canada. Students close to the area are greatly encouraged to find their own way to the venue. For students farther away, we'll be sending a bus through Toronto and Waterloo, if funding permits.

Code of Conduct?

Yes, of course! All hackers must abide by the MLH Code of Conduct found here.

Still have questions?

Send us an email at hello@masseyhacks.ca and we'll get back to you as soon as we can!
