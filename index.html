What is Massey Debate Invitationals?

Massey Debate Invitationals is a high school debate competition that takes place at Vincent Massey Secondary School. Debators of different skill levels from across the Windsor-Essex area come together for a day to brush up on their debating skills and have a great time! Our 2018 fall MDI is on the 24th of November. You may sign up with a partner online, but a $10 participation fee will be due upon entry. Hope to see you there!
